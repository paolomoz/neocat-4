PlusPoints earned upon achieving Premier Platinum and Premier 1K® status are now even more versatile. In addition to using PlusPoints to request premium seat and cabin upgrades, members will now be able to exchange eligible PlusPoints for various perks, including:

  • Gifting Premier status to other MileagePlus members
  • Premier qualifying points (PQP)
  • Premier qualifying flights (PQF)
  • TravelBank cash
  • Bonus miles

This benefit will be available for PlusPoints with an expiration date of 1/31/27 or later.
